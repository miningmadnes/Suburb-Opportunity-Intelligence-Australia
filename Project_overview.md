## Data Limitations

#### Different datasets use different location information:
- Crime data uses postcodes
- Construction permits and ABS data use statistical areas
- ABN and Education data does not have location
- Healthcare, National roads, Petrol stations and Railways stations use lat/long coordinates

#### Different datasets have different update dates
- ABS was last updated in 2021
